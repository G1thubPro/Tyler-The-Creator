About Tyler The Creator

Tyler, The Creator is an influential American rapper, producer, and designer known for his innovative approach to music and art. Born Tyler Gregory Okonma on March 6, 1991, in Ladera Heights, California, he gained prominence as the leader of the alternative hip-hop collective Odd Future, which formed in 2007. Tyler's unique style blends elements of hip-hop, jazz, and punk, often characterized by his introspective lyrics and bold, eclectic production.
